Boerenkool

Today we will learn how to make a typical dutch dish called Stampot Boerenkool It is easy to make, it is optional to make it with a meatball or a smoked sausage, but for this recipe we used the smoked sausage.

  • start boilet the potates
  • Boil the Cale
  • bake the smoked sausage
  • When both the cale and the potatos are boiled stamp them together
  • Add salt and pepper to tast
  • Make a gravy (optional)
  • Enjoy!
