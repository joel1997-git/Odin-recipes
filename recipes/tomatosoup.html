Tomato soup

This recipe is for a home made tomato soup for the cold winter days

  • Cut around 1000 grams of tomatos
  • Cut 1 large carrot
  • Boil this together for about 30 minutes
  • Add 2 blocks of maggi beef boulion
  • Blend everything together
  • Optional add a little bit of Thyme
  • Serve with bread
  • Enjoy!
