fried rice

This recipe is for Fried rice I made this recipe myself, it is a healthy form of fried rice with alot of veggies

  • Step one is to cut the chicken and the vegetables
  • For the next step we will take our Wok-pan and heat it up for about 5 minutes on high heat
  • When the Wok is hot enough we put some oil in the wok and start stir-frying our chicken
  • when our chicken is in the wok for about 5 minutes we will add our veggies
  • If the veggies and the chicken are cooked well enough we will add our (day old) rice
  • Add soy sauce, ketjap, black pepper, paprika powder, MSG, and fry the rice
  • Top it of with an egg and enjoy!
